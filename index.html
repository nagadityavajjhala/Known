EXHIBITIONS

Year 2025 — 2026
Type IB Visual Arts Exhibition
Venue Indus International School

"Power,
Politics & Emotions"

A multi-media exhibition exploring the raw intersections of political power, human emotion, and societal tension. The body of work spans graphite drawings, acrylic paintings on canvas, digital art, sculptural forms, and mixed-media installations — each piece serving as a confrontation, a question, or a declaration.

Developed as the capstone of my IB Visual Arts programme, the collection challenges viewers to examine the masks we wear and the invisible systems that govern us. Through recurring motifs — like the strategic hierarchy of chess pieces and the cyclical absurdity of a clown carousel — the work transforms abstract concepts into visceral, emotional landscapes. It is an invitation to look beyond the surface and engage with the chaotic beauty of the human condition.
